Key Takeaways

These are descriptive findings from the available leaderboard structure only. They should not be treated as causal conclusions.

1) The field is Rx-heavy. Most valid entries in this dataset are in the Rx division, making Rx the main competitive story.
2) Men and women participation is relatively balanced. The two gender groups are close in size, allowing clean side-by-side comparisons.
3) Percentile benchmarks are more useful than a single average. For athletes, coaches, and content viewers, percentile cutoffs communicate competitive standing better than raw mean score alone.
4) This file is best for leaderboard analysis, not gym or province analysis. Richer community insights would need athlete name, affiliate, location, age, and tie-break fields.
